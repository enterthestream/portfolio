Meditation

Practise daily and visit the International Meditation Centre UK for silent Vipassana retreats a few times a year to calm the mind, focus and mould habits for the future.

Simulation Racing

Race in a simulator on iRacing, using books and telemetry to train effectively. Came 3rd place in the iRacing Series Championship in my rookie reason and rated in the top 5% of almost 300k drivers.

Sport

Play county level football as a centre-back calm on the ball and pressing aggressively and decisively to thwart counter attacking opportunities, earning trophies with Henfield FC.

Baking and Coffee Brewing

Fermenting dough to make bread. Preparing high quality pour over coffee, praised by friends for having no bitterness and full of natural sweetness.

Film Photography and Film Cinema

Find candid street film photography of family, friends and travels on my curated instagram feed. Collecting and discussing films, especially old restored films by the likes of Criterion Collection and BFI.
